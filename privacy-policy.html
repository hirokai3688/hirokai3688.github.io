Japan MalaTang Monitor Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 10, 2026

Important: This Privacy Policy describes how Japan MalaTang Monitor collects, uses, and protects information obtained through TikTok's official API.

1. About Japan MalaTang Monitor

Japan MalaTang Monitor is a research tool developed to monitor and analyze Mala Tang related content trends on Japanese TikTok. The application uses TikTok's official API to collect publicly available data for academic and market research purposes.

2. Information We Collect

Japan MalaTang Monitor collects the following types of information through TikTok's public API:

2.1 Public Content Data

  • Video metadata (video IDs, descriptions, captions, hashtags)
  • Content engagement metrics (likes, comments, shares, views)
  • Publication dates and timestamps
  • Content categorization and tags

2.2 Public User Information

  • Public usernames and display names
  • Follower and following counts
  • Public profile information
  • Content posting patterns and frequencies

2.3 Analytics Data

  • Trend analysis data
  • Content performance metrics
  • Audience engagement patterns
  • Geographic content distribution

3. How We Use Collected Information

Japan MalaTang Monitor uses the collected data exclusively for:

  • Research Analysis: Studying Mala Tang content trends in the Japanese market
  • Academic Research: Supporting academic studies on social media trends
  • Market Insights: Providing insights into content performance and audience engagement
  • Tool Improvement: Enhancing the application's functionality and accuracy
  • Statistical Reporting: Generating anonymous, aggregated statistical reports

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Storage Practices

  • All data is stored on secure, encrypted servers
  • Data retention is limited to the period necessary for research purposes
  • Regular security audits and updates are performed
  • Access to stored data is restricted to authorized personnel only

4.2 Security Measures

  • Industry-standard encryption for data transmission and storage
  • Regular security vulnerability assessments
  • Secure access controls and authentication mechanisms
  • Compliance with data protection best practices

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

Japan MalaTang Monitor is committed to protecting user privacy:

  • No Sale of Data: We do not sell, trade, or rent personal information
  • Limited Sharing: We may share anonymous, aggregated data in research publications
  • Legal Compliance: We may disclose information when required by law
  • Service Providers: We may use trusted third-party services for hosting and analytics, all of whom are bound by confidentiality agreements

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your information:

  • Access Right: Request access to the data we have collected about your content
  • Correction Right: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion Right: Request deletion of your data from our systems
  • Opt-Out Right: Opt-out of data collection by contacting us
  • Data Portability: Request a copy of your data in a structured format

7. International Data Transfers

As Japan MalaTang Monitor operates across borders, data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your residence. We ensure such transfers comply with applicable data protection laws and implement appropriate safeguards.

8. Children's Privacy

Japan MalaTang Monitor does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information.
