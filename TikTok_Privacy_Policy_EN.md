# Privacy Policy

## 1. Introduction
OpenClaw Linky TikTok Assistant (hereinafter referred to as "the App") values user privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information through TikTok API.

## 2. Information Collection Scope
When you authorize the App to access your TikTok account, we collect the following information through TikTok Open API:

### 2.1 Basic Account Information
- TikTok User ID and username
- Profile information (avatar, display name, bio)
- Account verification status and type

### 2.2 Content Information
- Published video list and metadata
- Video titles, descriptions, hashtags
- Video statistics (views, likes, comments, shares)

### 2.3 Social Information
- Follower count statistics
- Following count statistics
- Fan interaction trend data

### 2.4 Usage Data
- App feature usage frequency
- Feature preference settings
- Service request logs (anonymized)

## 3. Purpose of Information Use
We use collected information only for:

### 3.1 Core Service Provision
- Generating content analysis reports
- Providing fan interaction insights
- Optimizing content publishing suggestions
- Personalized AI recommendations

### 3.2 Service Improvement
- Analyzing feature usage to optimize user experience
- Identifying and fixing technical issues
- Developing new features

### 3.3 Security and Compliance
- Detecting and preventing abusive behavior
- Ensuring service security and stability
- Complying with legal and platform requirements

## 4. Data Storage and Security

### 4.1 Storage Methods
- Data encrypted and stored on secure servers
- Sensitive information additionally encrypted
- Regular backups and disaster recovery

### 4.2 Retention Period
- Service data retained until user deletes account or revokes authorization
- Anonymized analytics data may be retained longer
- Log data regularly cleaned (maximum 90 days)

### 4.3 Security Measures
- HTTPS encrypted transmission
- Server access controls
- Regular security audits
- Employee privacy training

## 5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

### 5.1 We Will Not:
- Sell your personal data
- Share identifiable information with advertisers
- Use data for purposes not stated in this policy

### 5.2 Sharing Only in These Cases:
- **Legal Requirements**: Responding to court orders or legal proceedings
- **Protecting Rights**: Preventing fraud, abuse, or security threats
- **Service Providers**: Sharing with necessary cloud service providers (under confidentiality agreements)

## 6. User Rights and Control

### 6.1 Your Rights
- **Access Right**: View your data we hold
- **Deletion Right**: Request deletion of your personal data
- **Correction Right**: Correct inaccurate information
- **Withdrawal Right**: Revoke TikTok API access anytime

### 6.2 How to Exercise Rights
1. Manage data through in-app settings
2. Revoke app permissions via TikTok settings
3. Send request email to: hirokai3688@outlook.com

## 7. TikTok Platform Integration
- The App complies with TikTok Developer Policies and API Terms of Use
- You authorize data access through TikTok OAuth flow
- Authorization scope is clearly displayed on authorization page
- You can manage third-party app permissions anytime in TikTok settings

## 8. Children's Privacy
The App is not directed to children under 13. If we discover we have collected data from children, we will delete it immediately.

## 9. International Data Transfers
Data is primarily stored on servers in the operating region, which may involve cross-border transfers. We ensure transfers comply with applicable data protection laws.

## 10. Policy Updates
We may update this policy to reflect service changes or legal requirements. Significant changes will:
- Be notified through in-app notifications
- Update the "Last Updated" date at the top of the page
- Give users reasonable time to review changes
